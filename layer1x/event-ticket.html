California Art Festival: 2023 Dana Point 29-30 May one hour online session

Event Date
May 29, 2023
Location
California, USA
Event Type
Online Event

Event Description

The California Art Festival is a showcase of the best and brightest artists from across the state. From painters to sculptors, photographers to performance artists, this festival has it all. It's a chance for artists to come together and share their work with the world, and for art lovers to experience the beauty and diversity of California's art scene. One of the things that makes the California Art Festival so special is the incredible range of styles and mediums on display. You'll see everything from traditional oil paintings to cutting-edge digital art, from abstract sculptures to intricate installations.
