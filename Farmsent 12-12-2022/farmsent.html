Fair Future Contract (FFC) -

Fair Future Contract or FFC is a contract that doesnt lock the farmers. The farmers utilizing the FFC get to sell at the prevalent market price and not at the price determined months ago. Under FFC Farmsent arranges a soft contract that is not binding and in favor of the farmers.
